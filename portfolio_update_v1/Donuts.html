Philosophy of Donuts

Author unknown

  • Optimist: Sees the doughnut, not the hole.
  • Pessimist: Sees the hole, not the doughnut.
  • Realist: Sees both the doughnut and the hole.
  • Heraclitus "You can't eat the same donut twice."
  • Plato "All donuts share the same donut-ness."
  • Decartes "A donut's hole proves the donut's existence."
  • Locke "Donuts taste good to me."
  • Kant "A donut = my total experience of donuts."
  • Marx "Everybody deserves donuts."
  • Nietzsche "Stop at nothing to get your donuts."
